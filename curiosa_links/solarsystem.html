World's Largest Solarsystem Model

Stretches vertically over the whole country

Sweden has the largest model of our solarsystem in the whole world. It all starts in the Stockholm globe whitch is an arena that also represents the sun. The model stretches across the country crossing multiple citys along the way including Uppsala, Örebro, Umeå, Söderhamn and a few more. The scale of the model is 1 to 20 million
